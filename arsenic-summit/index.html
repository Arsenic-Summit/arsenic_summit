What is Arsenic Summit?

Arsenic Summit is an institutional, premium Model United Nations experience engineered for outcomes — not noise. We build rooms where research matters, negotiation is the currency, and visibility is earned through discipline. Delegates enter a sharper arena of diplomacy: pressure, persuasion, and leadership that survives scrutiny.
