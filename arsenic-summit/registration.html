Who can apply

School and college delegates can apply as individuals or as part of a delegation. Arsenic Summit matches delegates to rooms based on preference-fit, readiness, and committee balance.

Track

Individual

Apply as a solo delegate. Choose committee preferences and your experience level. We’ll allocate based on fit and room balance.

Track

Delegation

Schools/colleges can register delegations. We provide a single coordination lane, consolidated confirmations, and schedule clarity.

Step 1

Submit application

Fill preferences, experience, and delegate details. Write clearly. Avoid exaggeration.

Step 2

Receive review/allotment

We assess preference-fit and room balance. Allotment emails are sent in batches.

Step 3

Confirm participation

Pay the fee within the confirmation window. Seat is secured only after confirmation.

Step 4

Attend the summit

Arrive prepared. Deliver outcomes. Leave with credibility and documentation.
